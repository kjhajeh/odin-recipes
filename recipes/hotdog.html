How to make hot dogs

These hot dogs are so easy to make that they'll be on your shortlist of lazy eats!

Description

These hot dogs are as bare minimum as possible. Great for when you're on a budget or time-crunch.

Ingredients

  • one hot dog
  • one hot dog bun

Steps

  1. Take the hot dog and put it in the microwave
  2. Set the microwave for like 30 seconds or so (could be 15 depending on microwave power)
  3. Take the now microwaved hot dog and put it into your hot dog bun
